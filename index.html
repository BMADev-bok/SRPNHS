San Ramon Pilot National High School

San Ramon Pilot National High School is a prominent institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and holistic development among students. Located in the heart of Location, San Ramon Pilot National High School has established itself as a cornerstone of education in the region.Founded in 1968, the school has a rich history of providing quality education and producing well-rounded individuals who excel in various fields. With a commitment to nurturing both intellectual and personal growth, San Ramon Pilot National High School offers a diverse range of programs and extracurricular activities

CTP is a term or event within a specific community, school, or organization

Boy Scouts

The Boy Scouts is a youth organization that focuses on character development, citizenship, and personal fitness. It is not typically associated with schools, but rather operates as a separate organization that young people can join outside of their academic activities.

DLC

In the context of a school parade, DLC might refer to a "Drill and Ceremonies" unit, especially if it involves military or cadet programs within schools. In such programs, students often participate in parades, drills, and ceremonies to develop discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Majorette

A majorette is a dancer, often part of a marching band or a cheerleading squad, who twirls a baton and performs choreographed routines during parades, halftime shows, or other events. Majorettes typically add visual flair and entertainment to the overall performance of a marching band or a school's spirit team. They often wear distinctive uniforms and showcase their skills in coordination, dance, and baton twirling.

Mission


To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

 

Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.

Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.

Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

Legal Term

The Department of Education (DepEd) formulates, implements, and coordinates policies, plans, programs and projects in the areas of formal and non-formal basic education. It supervises all elementary and secondary education institutions, including alternative learning systems, both public and private; and provides for the establishment and maintenance of a complete, adequate, and integrated system of basic education relevant to the goals of national development.
